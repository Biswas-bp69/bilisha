Common Questions

FAQ

A few things people usually ask before we get started.

How do you handle project timelines?

I start every project with a scoping session to define milestones and realistic deadlines. I share progress regularly and flag any blockers early so there are never surprises.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I've worked with clients across Asia, Europe, and North America. Communication happens async via email and chat, with scheduled video calls when needed.

What does your typical process look like?

Discovery → Design → Development → Testing → Launch → Handoff. Each phase has a clear deliverable and sign-off before we move to the next.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes — I offer a 30-day bug fix period on all projects, and optional monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing updates and hosting management.
