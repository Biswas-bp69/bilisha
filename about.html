I'm Bilisha, a full-stack developer based in Kathmandu with a passion for crafting clean, performant, and user-centered digital products. My journey started with curiosity about how websites work, and over the years it grew into a career building everything from small business sites to complex dashboards.

I specialize in React and Node.js, and I'm comfortable across the entire stack — from designing components in Figma to writing REST APIs and deploying with Docker. I take pride in writing maintainable, well-documented code that teams actually enjoy working with.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually reading about design systems, contributing to open source, or experimenting with new front-end tooling. I care deeply about accessibility and the small details that make an interface feel effortless to use.

My Approach

How I Work

Principles I bring to every project, big or small.

Research First

Before writing a single line of code, I make sure I fully understand the problem. Good solutions come from deep understanding, not quick assumptions.

Iterate Fast

I believe in shipping early and improving often. Fast feedback loops lead to better products than months of work in isolation.

Every project I build follows WCAG guidelines. The web should work for everyone — accessibility is a baseline, not a bonus.
